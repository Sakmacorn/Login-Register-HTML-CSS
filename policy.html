5. Cookies 
            Migelana Online may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files 
            stored on your device that help us analyze how players use the game.

6. Your Rights 
            You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time.
            You can contact us for any privacy-related concerns or requests.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy 
            We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
            Please review it regularly to stay informed about our data practices.
